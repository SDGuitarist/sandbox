# Outreach Strategy Research

Research and findings from warm outreach campaigns. Source of truth for enrichment design, template effectiveness, and segment targeting patterns.

## Origin

April 20, 2026 outreach batch for Amplify Workshop (April 25). 30 personalized Facebook DMs sent to warm contacts. 3-layer pipeline:

1. **Lead sourcing (Facebook):** Manual search of Alex's friends list by industry keyword, filtered to San Diego. Produced 134 contacts across 6 segments.
2. **Enrichment (Perplexity):** Each contact researched via Perplexity AI search to find a verifiable recent public hook (property closing, podcast episode, article, event, award). 35 of 38 contacts had Perplexity-sourced openers.
3. **Voice templating (Claude Code):** Segment-specific templates written in Alex's voice, stitched with personalized openers. Each message = opener + template + soft CTA.

## Results

- 30 sent, 7 replied (23% response rate)
- 3 warm/hot leads (10% warm conversion)
- 3 declines (all produced value: referral, competitor intel, self-disqualification)
- 1 peer connection
