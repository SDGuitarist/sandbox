FC39: App Factory Created Per Job in Worker

Look for these specific patterns:

PASS if:
- create_app() is called ONCE at module level, in __main__, or at the top of the main loop
- The app instance is reused across all job iterations
- app.app_context() is used from the module-level app, not from a fresh create_app()
- The processing function receives the app or uses a module-level reference

FAIL if:
- create_app() is called inside the per-job processing function
- create_app() is called inside the polling loop body (not before the loop)
- Each job iteration creates a new Flask app instance
- The worker re-initializes blueprints, CSRF, rate limiter, etc. on every job
